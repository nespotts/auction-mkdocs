# Ring 2 — the pole-barn ring (sells all day out of a 42' × 72' building)

Ring 2 is the "everything else" ring: piles, group lots, and individual small machines sold rapid-fire all day. These move fast and are **bargain territory** — onsite buyers regularly steal small lots when the crowd is watching Ring 1. The listing doesn't itemize most of it, so values below are per-item ballparks; expect a lot of it to sell in box/group lots. **Watch for the two sleepers: the pro chainsaws and the lumber.**

**Ring-2 takeaway:** Individually small, but a patient onsite buyer can assemble a profitable pile here — especially the two Husqvarna pro saws (strong, liquid resale) and any hardwood/live-edge slabs (the one category that can quietly outrun its hammer price). Because Ring 2 runs concurrently, decide in advance whether you're chasing Ring 1 big-ticket lots or working Ring 2 deals — you can't be in both rings at once.
